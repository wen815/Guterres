Antonio Manuel de Oliveira Guterres

Born on April 30th 1949, in Lisbon, the capital of Portugal where he raised, Guterres is a politician who has ever worked as the General Secretary of Socialist Party in Portugal, the national secretary of Socialist Party and primary minister of Republic of Portugal.

In 2005, Guterres begins to lead the Office of United Nations High Commissioner for Refugees.

In 2017,Guterres begins to work as United Nations Secretary-General, with a duration of five years.
